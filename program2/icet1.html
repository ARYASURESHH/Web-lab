Welcome to ICET

Empowering Generation Through Excellence in Engineering Education since 2002

ICET has been one of the pioneering self financing colleges in Kerala. It is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University(KTU). The college was established in the year 2002. Within two decades since its inception, ICET has made many creditable and remarkable achievements. 21 batches of Engineers from various branches have been passed out from the college with academic brilliance and technological insight; and they occupy key positions in leading corporates and institutions. Many of our alumni have paved themselves worthwhile as successful entrepreneurs. With a strong commitment to social responsibility, ICET offers scholarships and free education through various agencies, empowering future leaders in engineering and technology.
